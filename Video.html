Short Films

Half Moon Night

This is a film that I worked on for RTF343 Advanced Narrative. It's a story about an old couple and the difficulties they face. I chose this story because I knew it would be hard on the actors and I wanted to challenge myself as a director.

Cafe in the Rain

I made this film for RTF318 Intro to Narrative Production. It's a story about a couple and how they first met. I chose this story because it was simple and enjoyable.

Spring Autumn Sketch

I made this film as my final project for the Liberal Arts Digital Storytelling Seminar. It's a story about a couple at three different points in their lives; when they were children, college age, and adult. It chose this story because it was difficult to tell and I knew I would need to take my production quality to the next level. The story was beautiful and painful and it needed non-linear storytelling and beautiful cinematography and music to match.

Economics Play

This was my first short film made for high school macroeconomics class. It's a story about a government official whose arrogance helped cause the Great Depression and the redemption he seeks from it. I chose this story because it had to be somewhat related to economics. Also our production value and timeframe made it so we had to do a mostly dialogue based story with minimal locations.
